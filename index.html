Six Ways to Savour Saffron

01

Kashmiri Noon Chai

Steep 6–8 threads in warm milk for 10 minutes. Add green tea, baking soda, and ice water — then the iconic pink bloom begins. Top with crushed pistachios.

02

Saffron Golden Latte

Dissolve 5 threads in 1 tsp warm water. Warm oat milk, add turmeric, ginger and your saffron bloom. A calming evening ritual with golden hues.

03

Biryani & Pulao

Soak 10–12 threads in 3 tbsp warm milk for 20 minutes. Drizzle over par-cooked rice before the final dum. Unlocks the legendary saffron colour and floral fragrance.

04

Persian-style Desserts

Saffron is essential in sheer khurma, phirni, and halwa. Infuse in warm sugar syrup first — colour distributes evenly and the earthy sweetness is unmatched.

05

Saffron Honey Drizzle

Warm raw honey gently, steep 8 threads until deep amber. Use on toast, yogurt, or cheese boards. Our pre-infused Saffron Honey is ready to use straight from the jar.

06

Cold Brew Saffron Tea

Add 10 threads to 500ml cold water. Refrigerate 6–8 hours. The result: a delicate amber drink with floral, grassy notes. Serve with lemon and honey.
